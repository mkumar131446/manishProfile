Talent Planning Process:

As the sole UI developer, I played a pivotal role in conceptualizing, developing, and implementing the Talent Planning Process, an internal application within the Human Resource Department during my tenure at Medline Industries. This project was built entirely from scratch, leveraging a robust technology stack that included Angular 15, SCSS, HTML, the company's style library UILine, and the powerful Ag-Grid. The application facilitated the efficient management of employee performance and potential rating points, streamlining collaboration between respective managers and HR professionals, and significantly enhancing the overall effectiveness of talent management within the organization.
